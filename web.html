Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science

Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science (MITS) was established with a vision to create a center of academic excellence in engineering, technology, and management. Located in Varikoli, Puthencruz, Kerala, the institute provides world-class infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories, and experienced faculty.
